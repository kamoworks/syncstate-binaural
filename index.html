The science

SyncState implements three expired, public-domain US patents as one layered system. US 5,356,368 (Monroe, 1994): binaural Frequency Following Response — two slightly different tones, one per ear, encourage the brain to synchronize to their difference. US 5,954,630 (Masaki, 1999): a low tone amplitude-modulated at 2–10 Hz stimulates frontal midline theta — the EEG signature of absorbed attention (works on speakers). US 5,245,666 (Mikell, 1993): affirmations held at the edge of hearing by real-time psychoacoustic masking.
