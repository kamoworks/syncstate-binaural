The science

SyncState implements the audio methods of US Patent 5,356,368 — Robert A. Monroe's "Method of and Apparatus for Inducing Desired States of Consciousness" (1994, now expired and in the public domain). Two pure tones, slightly different in each ear, cause the brain's Frequency Following Response to synchronize neural activity to the difference frequency — the binaural beat — encouraging hemispheric synchronization ("Hemi-Sync").
